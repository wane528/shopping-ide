## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does AutoZone sell windshield repair kits?**

Yes. AutoZone carries Permatex, Rain-X, Blue Star, 3M, and Versachem. Prices range from $9.99-$17.99. Staff can help you choose the right kit for your damage.

**Does Walmart have windshield repair kits?**

Yes. Walmart carries Rain-X and Permatex in most stores, plus their store brand. Prices are the lowest among brick-and-mortar retailers ($8.97-$14.97). Check the automotive section.

**Where can I buy a windshield repair kit near me?**

AutoZone, O'Reilly, Advance Auto Parts, Walmart, and NAPA all carry windshield repair kits. Use Google Maps or store websites to check local availability. Most auto parts stores have them in stock.

**Is it cheaper to buy online or in store?**

Online (Amazon) and Walmart offer the lowest prices. Auto parts stores (AutoZone, O'Reilly) are slightly higher but offer expert advice and rewards programs that can offset the difference.
